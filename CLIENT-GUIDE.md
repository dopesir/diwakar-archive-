# Editing your website — a simple guide

This site has a built-in editor (a "CMS"). You don't need to know any code. You
sign in, change words and pictures, click **Save**, and the website updates by
itself a minute later. You can't break the design — every option is a safe,
pre-set choice.

> **Golden rule:** if you're ever unsure, just don't click **Save** / **Publish**.
> Nothing changes on the live site until you do.

---

## 1. Getting in

1. Go to **your-site-address/admin/** (e.g. `…pages.dev/admin/`).
2. Click **Sign in with GitHub** and log in.
3. You'll see the list of things you can edit down the left side.

---

## 2. The big idea

- The **menu (top bar) and the footer** are fixed — they're not editable here
  (on purpose, so they always look right).
- **Everything else** is editable: the words, the photos, the order of things,
  which sections show, and some look-and-feel choices.
- Anything you leave blank quietly falls back to a sensible default. Empty never
  means broken.

---

## 3. Common things you'll want to do

### Edit a Story / Work / Thought
Left side → **Stories** (or Work / Thoughts) → click the item → change the text
or photo → **Save**.

### Add a new one
Same collections → **New** (top right) → fill it in → **Save**. Tip: leave
"Hide from site" off when you want it visible.

### Re-arrange the order of items (like the home slideshow)
Every collection has an **Order** number. Lower numbers come first.
- The **home slideshow** always follows its Order.
- For the **Work / Stories / Thoughts grids**, first open **Home page sections →
  (that grid) → Sort = Manual**, then set each item's **Order**. (If Sort is
  "Newest" or "By year", the site sorts automatically and ignores Order.)

### Hide a section, or change its order on the home page
Left side → **Home page sections** → toggle **Show** off, or change its
**Order**. The section's design stays the same; you're only showing/hiding or
moving it.

### Change a section's look (alignment, width, spacing, background)
**Home page sections** → pick the section → choose from the drop-downs
(Align, Width, Spacing, Background). These are safe presets — a dark background
automatically uses light text, so it's always readable.

### Put up an announcement bar (e.g. "Exhibition this Friday")
**Site settings → Site & SEO → Announcement bar** → turn it **On**, type the
message (and an optional link) → **Save**. Turn it Off to remove it.

### Change a photo or its caption
Open the item → click the image field → upload a new picture. Add **Alt text**
(a short description of the photo — good for accessibility and Google). For
photos that get cropped, **Focal point** lets you pick which part stays in view.

### Schedule something for later
Set a **Publish date** in the future and it stays hidden until then (it appears
on the next site rebuild after that date).

---

## 4. Languages (English / हिंदी)
The site is ready for Hindi, but the language switcher is **hidden until you have
Hindi content**. When you're ready to offer Hindi, turn it on under
**Site settings → Site & SEO → Languages**. Until then, leave it off.

---

## 5. How changes go live
When you click **Save/Publish**, your change is recorded and the website rebuilds
itself automatically — it's usually live within about a minute. Refresh the page
to see it.

If anything ever looks wrong, tell your developer — every change is saved in
history and can be undone.
