Imagine Photojournalist Society

Founded 2012 · Jaipur, India · Camera Commandos Initiative

Founded by Purushottam Diwakar, the Imagine Photojournalist Society is dedicated to training the next generation of visual storytellers — with a special focus on women in crisis journalism.

The “Camera Commandos” programme trains female photojournalists to document conflict zones, natural disasters, and human-interest stories. Seminars and field training have shaped hundreds of photographers across Rajasthan.
