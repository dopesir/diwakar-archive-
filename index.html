INDIA, 1987—
The Eye Behind the Frame

Purushottam Diwakar has served as an unflinching witness to the world’s most defining conflicts and quietest dignities. As a Senior Photojournalist for the India Today Group since 1998, his lens has captured the raw, unfiltered reality of war zones, ensuring the human cost of conflict is never forgotten.

Beyond the front lines, his life’s work is deeply rooted in documenting rural movements. He has spent decades chronicling the struggles, resilience, and unyielding spirit of grassroots agrarian communities. From drought-stricken landscapes to mass tribal mobilizations, his archive is a testament to the power of the people.
