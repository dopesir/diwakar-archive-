title: "The Tribe That Wanted to Kill Me"
date: "Remote Africa · 2017"
year: 2017
location: "Remote Africa"
---

During an expedition in remote Africa, Diwakar misinterpreted a tribe’s gestures. 'They wanted to kill me.' He escaped without a single frame. 'Survival matters more than the shutter.'

He came away with nothing to show for the journey — no frame, no record of the encounter. The story he keeps is about the limit of the work, the point at which leaving is the only choice.
