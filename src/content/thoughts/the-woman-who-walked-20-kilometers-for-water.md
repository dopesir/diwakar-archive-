date: "Jaisalmer · 2019"
year: 2019
location: "Jaisalmer"
---

He followed a woman named Geeta for two days. 'Her feet were cracked, but she never complained. That portrait was never published — at her request.'

Over two days the walk repeated, the same distance covered for the same water. The portrait exists but stays unpublished, held back at the request of the person in it.
