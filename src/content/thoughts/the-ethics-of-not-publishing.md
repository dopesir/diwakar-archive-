title: "The Ethics of Not Publishing"
date: "India Today Archives"
year: 2010
location: "India Today Archives"
---

He once captured a moment so negative for society that he chose not to disclose it. 'I had to choose not to communicate the negativity.' The image remains locked in his private archive.

The decision was not about whether the picture was true but about what publishing it would do. It stays in the archive as a record of a choice rather than of an event.
