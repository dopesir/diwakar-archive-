---
title: "The Unseen Portrait of a Beggar"
date: "Delhi · 2011"
year: 2011
location: "Delhi"
quote: "“Commodifying grief is not journalism.”"
---

He spent an afternoon with an elderly man near India Gate. 'He told me his son had thrown him out. I took his portrait but never published it. Some pain is not for sale.'

The afternoon produced a portrait he decided the man’s circumstances did not entitle him to sell. It remains unpublished — a picture made and then deliberately set aside.
