He waited all summer for the first rain. When it finally arrived, he cried behind the camera. 'A farmer raised his arms to the sky — that image became a symbol of hope.'

The whole summer had been a wait — for the sky to change and for the work to begin again. The frame caught the instant relief arrived, held in a single raised figure.
