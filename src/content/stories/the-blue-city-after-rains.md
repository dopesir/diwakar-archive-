After an unseasonal downpour, Diwakar walked through the soaked lanes of Jodhpur. 'The blue walls reflected the grey sky — it was like walking inside a watercolor.'

The rain changed the city he knew, deepening the indigo of the walls and pooling the light in the lanes. The frames keep that brief, altered version of Jodhpur before it dried back to ordinary day.
