title: "The Boatman Who Saved My Camera"
date: "Varanasi · 2014"
year: 2014
location: "Varanasi"
quote: "“Sometimes a stranger understands your purpose.”"
mood: "water"
---

His boat capsized. A boatman dove in and rescued his camera bag before Diwakar himself. 'He said, ‘Your eye is more important than your life.’'

A stranger on the river understood, in an instant, what the bag meant to the man who carried it. The story stays with the work as a reminder of that unasked-for help.
