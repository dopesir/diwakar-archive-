During the Kerala floods, he captured rescue operations. 'A fisherman used his boat to save dozens. That image of him carrying a child became a symbol of humanity.'

The rescues were carried out by people who simply had boats and chose to use them. The frame of a fisherman lifting a child came to stand for that ordinary courage.
