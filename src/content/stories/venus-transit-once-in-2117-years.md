He photographed the rare transit of Venus across the Sun. 'I knew I was framing history for generations unborn.' The image later appeared in astronomy journals.

The event itself lasted only hours and will not return within any living memory. What remains is the single frame — a small dark disc against the sun, kept for a future that has not yet arrived.
