quote: "“Wilderness accepts all scars.”"

A tigress injured by a trap roamed the buffer zone. 'She was majestic despite her wound. That image taught me that imperfection is its own beauty.'

She carried her injury without losing her bearing, moving through the buffer zone as though it were unremarkable. The frame keeps that composure rather than the wound.
